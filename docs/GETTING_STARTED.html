StartD8 SDK · Getting Started

From an idea to a working app — one guided path.

StartD8 walks your team from an empty folder to a real, running application. AI stakeholder personas help you decide what to build; a deterministic engine then builds most of it for free — and you approve every change along the way.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 AI personas draft your inputs 💸 ~89% of your app for $0 ✅ You approve every change

The whole journey, at a glance

There's a natural order to getting started — each tool hands off to the next. You don't need to learn them all up front; just follow the path. Here's the map, then a short "why it matters" for each stop.

1 Set up →  2 Understand →  3 Get guided →  4 Draft with AI personas →  5 Build (free) →  6 Approve →  7 Ship

The path — in the order you'll use it

Each step: the problem it removes, the value you get, and the one command to run.

1
Set up your project

Make it a StartD8 project

The problem

You have a folder and an idea — but no clear "start here."

What you get

One command turns your directory into a set-up StartD8 project — ready to build on, safe to re-run, and $0.

Do
startd8 project init
2
Understand your project

Concierge & Kickoff

Two names, one job. The Concierge is the engine — it surveys your project, tells you what's ready, and scaffolds the inputs your app needs. Kickoff is your cockpit over it — chat, terminal, or web. You'll usually just open Kickoff; the Concierge does the work underneath.
The problem

The SDK needs to know what your app is for — its goals, conventions, and who its stakeholders are.

What you get

A plain read on what's ready vs. missing, and a scaffolded starting point — including the stakeholder roster that powers the AI personas in the next step.

Do
startd8 concierge assess # the engine · or open the cockpit: startd8 kickoff
👩‍💻 For your engineers: startd8 concierge is the deterministic front door; startd8 kickoff concierge / concierge-chat are interactive surfaces over the same engine.
3
Never wonder "what's next?"

The Red Carpet

The problem

Big projects are overwhelming — what do I do first, then next?

What you get

A guided, staged conductor that always shows your next best action — and the exact command to run it. It's the through-line that sequences everything below.

Do
startd8 kickoff red-carpet
4
Let AI personas draft what your app needs

The Persona Panels

The problem

Blank-page paralysis: what are our goals, our requirements, our screens? Getting stakeholders in a room is slow.

What you get

Three sibling tools spin up synthetic stakeholder personas (AI agents role-playing your roster) that draft the inputs for you to approve. Free to start, opt-in AI for the drafting pass — and they draft structure and spec, never your real content.

Stakeholder Panel

Personas draft your value inputs — goals, conventions, targets.
startd8 panel recommend

Requirements Panel

Role-based personas draft candidate requirements (a spec to approve).
startd8 requirements elicit

Manifest Suggester

A designer persona drafts your screens — dashboards & pages.
startd8 screens suggest
🖊️ You stay the author. Every persona output is a labeled draft staged for your review — approve, edit, or reject. Nothing ships without your yes.
5
Build the real thing — for free

The $0 Generate Cascade

The problem

Generating an app is usually slow and expensive — every step burns AI cost.

What you get

From a single data contract, StartD8 builds a real, working app — data model, pages, forms, CRUD, dashboards — deterministically, at $0 AI cost. That's roughly 89% of your app with no per-run bill.

Do
startd8 generate backend # then: scaffold · views · polish
6
Stay in control

VIPP — approve changes on your terms

The problem

Automated changes to your project can feel risky — what exactly is being changed?

What you get

Proposed changes land in an inbox you control. Preview everything, then apply only what you approve — at your privilege, with nothing hidden. (It can even consult the stakeholder personas as it goes.)

Do
startd8 vipp negotiate → startd8 vipp apply
7
Go live

Deploy — ship it

The problem

Getting from "runs on my machine" to actually deployed is its own project.

What you get

A graded path installs, boots, health-checks, and smoke-tests your app — so you ship with confidence, not crossed fingers.

Do
startd8 deploy local

Why teams choose StartD8

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 AI where it adds value

Synthetic stakeholder personas draft your goals, requirements, and screens — so you start from a strong draft, not a blank page.

💸 Mostly free to build

The heavy lifting — data model, pages, forms, dashboards — is generated deterministically at $0 AI cost. You pay for AI only where it truly helps.

🧭 Guided, not guesswork

Every step hands off to the next and tells you what to do. New team members ramp up fast; nobody stares at a blank page.

✅ You're always in control

Nothing changes your project without your say-so. Preview, approve, apply — on your terms, with a full trail.
